# Nubia
## Kush
* trading partner and rival of Egypt
* closely associated with Egypt, influenced by their culture and religion
## Napata period
* vibrant perion of nubian history - heavily influenced by Egypt
* Napatan king Piye conquered Egypt
* Napatans ruled Egypt until they were expelled by the Assyrians
## Meroe
* Meroe was a major industrila center, producing a lot of iron
* Red Sea trade in gold, slaves, exotic luxury goods
* last Nubian civilization

# Axum (Ethiopia)
* Axum was a major city and trade center
* blend of native and Yemeni cultures
* spoke/wrote Ge'ez
* connection to ancient Israel
    * Queen of Sheba met with Solomon
    * she was said to have come back pregnant
* kingdom established by Menelik I, son of Queen of Sheba
* much later, Ethiopia converted to christianity during the reign of Ezzana
* Ethiopia rose to prominence in the region during this time
# Bantu expansion into sub-equitorial Africa
* use of iron tools spread throughout africa
* spread agriculture
